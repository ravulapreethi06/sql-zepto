Zepto Sales & Inventory Analysis (SQL + Power BI)
Business Problem:
Zepto wants to analyze its product-level sales, inventory, and discounts to identify factors affecting revenue and stock performance.

Objectives:

Identify top-performing product categories and underperformers
Analyze discount patterns and their impact on total revenue
Track out-of-stock trends and stock value across regions
Tools Used:

SQL: For data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation
Power BI: For interactive dashboard and insights visualization
Process Overview:

Extracted and cleaned raw data using SQL (joins, aggregations, CASE, CTEs).
Built key metrics in Power BI:
Total Revenue
Average Discount (%)
Total Products
Out-of-Stock Count
Total Stock Value
Created category-wise and region-wise dashboards for better decision-making.
Key Insights:

Platinum and Gold customers contributed ~65% of total sales.
Ineffective discount strategy reduced profit margins in key categories.
South region shows frequent out-of-stock issues — opportunity to optimize inventory.
Business Impact:
Optimizing discount strategies and inventory can increase profit margins and reduce lost sales due to stockouts.
